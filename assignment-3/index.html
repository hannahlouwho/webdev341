Pizza Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 6 chicken boullun cubes
  • 1 whole chopped onion
  • 3/4 tsp garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 2 cups velveeta cubed
  • 2 cups butter
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • 1 can choped olives
  • Canadian bacon
  • Peperoni
  • Hamb
  • Sausage
  • Peppers
  • Bread!
  • Anything else your heart desires

Directions

  1. Boil the first three ingredients.
  2. Add garlic salt and pepper.
  3. Mix one cup water and flour and add to boiling mixture
  4. Add half and half, velveeta, and butter. Mix well.
  5. Mix all other ingredients. Mix well.
  6. Let simmer for 10 minutes
  7. DONE!
  8. Serve with bread, or don't, whatever.
  9. But seriously, serve with bread.
